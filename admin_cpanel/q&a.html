ABOUT ORCA

During the final stages of development of Beyond The Arch, ORCA (Office of the Registrar Chatbot Assistant) was initially conceived as a course project by the members of TIDE during their last semester in the university. Months after their graduation, the members set out to complete this project as their gift to the Office of the Registrar.

Made during their post-college era with love.
